To Reach Thirupathi


Tirupati is well connected by Road, Rail and Air. Pilgrims can travel through any mode of transport available (Bus/Train/Flight), as operated by the respective agencies (RTCs/ Indian Railways/ Airlines/ or any private mode of transport) from time to time.

Regular connects to Renigunta and Gudur Railway Stations make it easy for Pilgrims to reach Tirupati within 30 minutes and 1 hr 30 minutes of travel time respectively.

Pilgrims may also land in Chennai or Bangalore Airports and drive down to Tirupati within three and five hours of travel time respectively.
